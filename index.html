Growing Method Tips

This Method Is Only Applicable For Growing Oyster Mushroom.

STEP 1

Get The Straw And Keep It In A Soution Of Water, Bevistine And Formaldehyde For Over Night.

STEP 2

Drain Out All The Water From The Tub And Let It Dry.

STEP 3

Take PP Bag And By Layer-Layer Add Straw And Mushroom Spawn Till the Neck Of The Bag And Tie It.

STEP 4

Now After Some Days Make Holes For Fruiting of Mushroom.

FINISH

Now Sprinkle Water Every Two Times In A Day.
